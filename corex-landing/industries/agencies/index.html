Where the Margin Goes

Where agencies lose
margin and clients.

📦

Scope Creep & Unpaid Work

Client requests grow beyond the brief. The team delivers everything asked. None of the extras get billed. Margin erodes one "quick favour" at a time.

  • Every scope addition tracked and flagged before delivery
  • Out-of-scope work requires approval before it's started
  • Retainer usage visible to account managers in real time
📊

Utilisation Blindness

No real-time view of who's overloaded and who has capacity. New work accepted that can't be delivered properly. Burnout follows. Quality suffers.

  • Real-time utilisation dashboard across every team member
  • Capacity checked before new work is sold in
  • Resourcing decisions made on data, not guesswork
📧

Manual Reporting

Weekly and monthly reports built manually. Hours pulling data from multiple platforms. Inconsistent quality. High-effort, low-value work that shouldn't need a human.

  • Data pulled automatically from ad platforms, analytics, social tools
  • Reports assembled and delivered on schedule without manual effort
  • Consistent format and quality across every client
🔀

Delivery Handoff Failures

Work moves between team members without structured handoffs. Context gets lost. Deadlines get missed. Clients feel the friction even when the output is good.

  • Project handoffs with full context preserved automatically
  • Internal briefings triggered at every stage change
  • Client approvals and feedback captured in one place
