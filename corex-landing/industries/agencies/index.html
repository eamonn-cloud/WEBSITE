Common Problems

Where agencies lose
margin and clients.

📦

Scope Creep & Unpaid Work

Client requests grow beyond the brief. The team delivers everything asked. None of the extras get billed. Margin erodes one "quick favour" at a time.

  • Scope change request workflows
  • Automated out-of-scope flagging
  • Retainer usage tracking and alerts
📊

Utilisation Blindness

No real-time view of who's overloaded and who has capacity. New work accepted that can't be delivered. Team burnout follows. Client quality suffers.

  • Real-time utilisation dashboards
  • Capacity forecasting for new pitches
  • Resource allocation and scheduling systems
📧

Client Reporting Overhead

Monthly and weekly reports built manually. Hours spent collating data from multiple platforms. Report quality inconsistent. High-effort, low-value work that shouldn't need humans.

  • Automated reporting workflows
  • Multi-platform data aggregation
  • Branded report generation and delivery
🔀

Delivery & Handoff Failures

Work moves between team members without structured handoffs. Context is lost. Deadlines are missed. Clients feel the gaps even when the output is good.

  • Project management architecture
  • Internal handoff and briefing workflows
  • Client approval and feedback systems
