Common Problems

Where professional services
firms lose money.

🐌

Slow Client Onboarding

Manual intake processes, information-gathering emails, document chasing — a new client takes two weeks to start. Every week of delay is a week of unbilled time.

  • Automated client intake workflows
  • Self-service document portals
  • CRM-linked onboarding checklists
💸

Unbilled Time & Scope Creep

Work gets done that isn't invoiced. Scope creep happens without authorisation. Billing cycles are manual and delayed. Revenue is being left on the table constantly.

  • Automated time capture and billing triggers
  • Scope change request workflows
  • Invoice automation and payment chasing
📊

Zero Capacity Visibility

No one knows who's overloaded and who has capacity. New work is accepted without understanding the delivery impact. Burnout and missed deadlines follow.

  • Real-time utilisation dashboards
  • Capacity planning tools
  • Workload forecasting and alerts
🔄

Broken Delivery Handoffs

Work moves between people, departments, or contractors without structured handoffs. Context is lost. Quality suffers. Clients feel the gaps even if they can't articulate them.

  • Project handoff automation
  • Internal knowledge transfer workflows
  • Client milestone communication systems
