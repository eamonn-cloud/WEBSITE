Our Approach

AI Readiness
Framework

Before any AI implementation, we assess your business across five dimensions. This determines where AI will create leverage - and where it will create noise.

01

Data Quality

AI is only as good as the data it processes. We audit your data structures, completeness, and accessibility before designing any AI layer.

02

Process Clarity

AI automates defined processes. If the process is unclear, AI amplifies the chaos. We document and standardise before automating.

03

Integration Readiness

AI must connect to your existing systems. We assess API availability, data flows, and integration architecture for each target system.

04

Team Adoption

AI tools unused are ROI-negative. We build adoption plans, training, and human-in-the-loop controls that make AI feel like help, not threat.

05

Governance & Controls

Clear ownership, access controls, monitoring, and review processes so AI operates within defined boundaries and can be audited at any time.
